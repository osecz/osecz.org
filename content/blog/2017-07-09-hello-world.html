Welcome to OSECZ.org. This website and blog is the home of the OSECZ community.

Our community originated at #osecz channel of Freenode IRC Network in January 2017. This community was formed to promote discussion and collaboration on open source security projects, security tools, protocols and standards. Some of the initial members of the channel were members of the long-established ##security channel of Freenode who were looking for a tiny, troll-free and friendly community. You are welcome to join this channel and be a part of the community. You are also welcome to invite your friends and colleagues to this channel and help the channel grow.

It has been about six months since the channel was formed and we thought that it would be nice to have a home for our community on the web. This website and this blog is an attempt at this.

We have the following goals in growing our channel and creating this website.

  1. Bring information security professionals to a common open platform to share knowledge with each other and collaborate on security projects.
  2. Encourage students and technology professionals in gaining expertise in information security.
  3. Increase general awareness of information security among non-technical folks.

In order to achieve these goals, we are expanding our network to social media channels such as Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook. We post our updates and general security news at these places. If you use one or more of these websites, please follow us there.
