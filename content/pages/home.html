Introduction

OSECZ stands for Open Security Zone. OSECZ is a collaboration area to discuss and work on projects related to open source tools, technologies, protocols and standards used in cybersecurity.

IRC Channel

Our IRC channel is #osecz on the Freenode IRC network.

For more details about how to connect to our IRC channel, please visit our IRC User Guide.
